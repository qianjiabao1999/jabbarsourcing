FAQ

These are the questions buyers ask most often. Final details can be confirmed after your inquiry.

Do you charge commission or service fees?

No. We charge buyers 0% commission. The quoted price is the price you receive, with no hidden fees.

Is there a minimum order amount?

First cooperation supports trial orders from USD 1,000; regular orders start from USD 3,000 and support mixed-category wholesale.

How do inspection and quality control work?

A dedicated specialist follows the order, opens cartons and packages one by one, and sends photos and videos in real time for your confirmation before shipping.

Which payment methods are supported?

We support Alipay payments and USD account transfers through Alibaba.com Pay.

How long does it take to receive a quote?

After you submit an inquiry, we reply with a complete quotation within 72 hours.

Can shipments be consolidated?

Yes. Consolidated container orders start from USD 10,000.

Do you support cross-border e-commerce dropshipping?

No. We focus on wholesale sourcing consolidation and FCL/LCL shipping, and do not provide one-piece dropshipping.
